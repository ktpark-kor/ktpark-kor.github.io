Process Systems and Simulation Laboratory

has been established at Sookmyung Women's University since 2018.

The Process Systems and Simulation Laboratory (PSSL), affiliated with the Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering at Sookmyung Women's University, specializes in cutting-edge research on the design, optimization, and sustainability of chemical process systems. The lab integrates artificial intelligence and advanced simulation techniques to develop innovative and environmentally responsible process solutions.
